ISSUE 01

Our first issue features our conversation with Charlotte von Hardenburgh—a historian, researcher, and designer based in Brooklyn—whose diverse influences shape her approach to fashion. Sealed in an envelope with Charlotte's Celine purse are bookmarks highlighting rising brands, photo cards of New Yorkers and their street fashion, stickers, and hand-pressed pins.

All components were designed, printed, and hand-assembled by Loose Buttons.
